## Local Pattern Analysis

### 1. Visual Elements — What Locals Use

| Pattern | Frequency | vs. National Leaders |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| **WordPress template** | 10/10 | Nationals: custom or heavily customized themes |
| **Real photography** | 5/10 | Nationals: 9/10 use real photos |
| **Stock/icon-heavy** | 5/10 | Nationals: 1/10 rely on stock |
| **Video content** | 0/10 | Nationals: 5/10 have video |
| **Custom typography** | 0/10 | Nationals: 3/10 use custom fonts |
| **Animated counters** | 1/10 (Clean Up Tiel) | Nationals: 4/10 |
| **Card-based services** | 6/10 | Nationals: 9/10 |
| **Generous whitespace** | 3/10 | Nationals: 8/10 |

### 2. Social Proof / Reviews

| Approach | Local Frequency | National Frequency |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Named testimonials | 5/10 (but avg 2-3 each) | 7/10 (avg 5+ each) |
| Star/number ratings | 2/10 | 4/10 |
| Client logo carousels | 2/10 | 6/10 |
| Certification badges | 2/10 (VCA, SIEV) | 5/10 (ISO, VCA, Keurmerk, CO2) |
| Zero social proof | 3/10 | 0/10 |
| Google/platform reviews | 1/10 | 2/10 |

### 3. Primary CTA Position

| Position | Local Frequency | National Frequency |
|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Top navigation (sticky) | 5/10 | 9/10 |
| Hero section | 4/10 | 8/10 |
| Repeated 3+ times | 3/10 | 7/10 |
| Contact form ON homepage | 2/10 | 3/10 |
| Phone click-to-call prominent | 4/10 | 5/10 |
| **No clear CTA above fold** | **4/10** | **0/10** |

### 4. Imagery Approach

| Approach | Local | National |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Real employee/project photos | 5/10 | 8/10 |
| Stock/generic imagery | 3/10 | 1/10 |
| Icon/illustration only | 4/10 | 0/10 (supplement only) |
| Video | 0/10 | 5/10 |
| Before/after photos | 0/10 | 1/10 |

### 5. Common Homepage Structure (Local)

Most local sites follow a thinner version:
```
1. Header with logo + nav (sometimes phone)
2. Hero slider or banner (often generic)
3. Service cards (3-4 items, brief)
4. About/value proposition paragraph
5. Maybe testimonials (1-3)
6. Footer with contact info
```

**Missing vs. national**: Key metrics, client logos, "why choose us" section, video, blog/news, FAQ, detailed CTAs.

### 7. Motion / Animation

### The Standout Local Competitor

**Clean Up Tiel** is the clear outlier — the only local competitor approaching national standards:
- 9.7/10 rating with 47 real reviews
- 25+ named testimonials
- Client logos displayed
- SIEV MKB certification
- Modern Elementor design with real photography
- Dual CTA approach (business vs residential)
- 12 sector specializations clearly presented

**However, even Clean Up Tiel lacks**: video content, animated counters, a truly custom design, and the depth of trust signals seen in nationals like TopCleaners or Balans.

## The "Local Baseline" — What Separates National Leaders from Local Competitors

### The Gap Summary

| Dimension | Local Baseline | National Standard | Gap |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|-----|
| **Design** | WordPress template, minimal customization | Custom/heavily customized, consistent design system | Large |
| **Photography** | 50% stock or icons, varying quality | 90% real, professional, brand-consistent photos | Large |
| **Video** | 0% have video | 50% embed corporate/team videos | Total |
| **Social proof** | 0-3 testimonials, rarely with ratings | 5-25+ testimonials, prominent ratings, client logos | Large |
| **Certifications** | 20% show any badge | 50%+ display ISO, VCA, Keurmerk, CO2 | Medium |
| **CTA strategy** | 1 vague CTA, often buried | "Offerte aanvragen" 3-4x, hero + nav + mid + footer | Large |
| **Homepage depth** | 5-6 thin sections | 10-12 rich sections with clear flow | Large |
| **Content strategy** | Static brochure, no blog | Blog/news, case studies, FAQ sections | Large |
| **Mobile UX** | Basic responsive (template default) | Mobile-optimized with touch CTAs, click-to-call, WhatsApp | Medium |
| **Animation** | Sliders or nothing | Subtle counters, hover effects, smooth transitions | Small |
| **SEO/Schema** | Basic or none | Rich LocalBusiness schema, FAQ schema, structured data | Large |
