## Pattern Analysis

### 1. Visual Elements That Appear in Most Successful Sites

| Pattern | Frequency | Details |
|---------|-----------|---------|
| **Card-based service layouts** | 9/10 | Services displayed as clickable cards with icons or images |
| **Real photography (not stock)** | 9/10 | Authentic photos of actual employees, equipment, and job sites |
| **Clean, generous whitespace** | 8/10 | Modern layouts with breathing room between sections |
| **Dark/navy + accent color palette** | 7/10 | Professional dark tones with green, blue, or teal accents |
| **Custom icon systems** | 7/10 | SVG/custom icons for service categories and value propositions |
| **Sans-serif typography** | 10/10 | Poppins, Roboto, Open Sans, Gilroy — no serif fonts |

### 2. How They Display Social Proof / Reviews

| Approach | Used By | Notes |
|----------|---------|-------|
| **Named testimonials with ratings** | TopCleaners, Balans, Boen Groen | Full quotes with client name, company, and star/number rating |
| **Client logo carousels** | CSU, Vebego, EW, TopCleaners, DES, Balans | 5-24+ logos of recognizable brands |
| **Certification badges** | CSU, Succes, Balans | ISO, VCA, Keurmerk Schoon, CO2 certificates |
| **Statistics/counters** | CSU, TopCleaners, Balans, Schoonmaakwoede | "328,562 windows cleaned", "100,000 homes", "99.6% satisfaction" |
| **Media mentions** | Schoonmaakwoede | RTL 4, Het Parool, Volkskrant logos |
| **Awards prominently displayed** | CSU | MT500 #1, Koning Willem I Prijs |
| **Employee spotlights** | Vebego, Succes | Named employee stories build human trust |
| **No reviews at all** | All Clean, EW | Relies on brand presence alone |

### 3. Primary CTA Position

| Position | Frequency |
|----------|-----------|
| **Top navigation (sticky)** | 9/10 |
| **Hero section** | 8/10 |
| **Repeated 3+ times on page** | 7/10 |
| **Contact form ON homepage** | 3/10 (TopCleaners, Balans, Schoonmaakwoede) |
| **Footer** | 6/10 |

**Dominant CTA text:** "Offerte aanvragen" (Request Quote) — used by 9/10 competitors.

### 4. Imagery Approach

| Approach | Frequency | Notes |
|----------|-----------|-------|
| **Real employee photos** | 8/10 | Authentic, in-action shots dominate |
| **Embedded video** | 5/10 | Corporate/culture videos (CSU, EW, Succes, TopCleaners, Balans) |
| **Icon/illustration heavy** | 3/10 | Schoonmaakwoede, Boen Groen, DES supplement with icons |
| **Stock photography** | 1/10 | Almost entirely avoided by successful competitors |
| **Group team photos** | 3/10 | TopCleaners, Balans, Succes show team |

### 5. Homepage Structure (Common Pattern)

Most successful sites follow this flow:

```
1. Sticky nav with "Offerte aanvragen" CTA
2. Hero: strong headline + background image/video + CTA button
3. Key metrics / trust numbers (employees, clients, satisfaction score)
4. Service cards (3-6 categories as clickable tiles)
5. Client logos carousel
6. "Why choose us" value propositions (3-4 pillars with icons)
7. Testimonials / reviews section
8. Company video or about section
9. Latest news / blog posts (3 items)
10. Final CTA + contact section
11. Comprehensive footer
```

## Key Takeaways for John's Schoonmaakbedrijf

1. **"Offerte aanvragen" is the universal CTA** — this must be prominent in nav, hero, and repeated throughout
2. **Real photography is non-negotiable** — stock photos immediately signal "small/amateur"
3. **Trust signals make or break conversion** — at minimum: testimonials with names, certification badges, and either client logos or stats
4. **The homepage should follow the proven structure**: hero → metrics → services → logos → why-us → testimonials → CTA
5. **Video builds trust** — even a simple 60-second "meet the team" video is used by 50% of top competitors
6. **Clean, professional sans-serif typography** with generous whitespace is the industry standard
7. **Mobile-first is mandatory** — all competitors are fully responsive
8. **Animations should be subtle** — counter animations and smooth hover states, nothing flashy
9. **Green/teal + dark tones** are the dominant color scheme in this industry (eco + professional)
10. **FAQ sections boost SEO** — several competitors use structured FAQ accordions with schema markup
